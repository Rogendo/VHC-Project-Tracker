Vihiga Project Tracker is an Information Management System designed for Vihiga County, located in the Western parts of Kenya. It keeps records of the diverse construction and development projects within the county. This system significantly reduce workloads and streamlines paperwork, ensuring enhanced efficiency. Moreover, it revolutionalizes project tender allocation by fostering transparency. Project progress, more information on the projects and the funds allocated will be readily accessible to the general public, promoting integrity,equity, accountability,transparency and inclusivity
